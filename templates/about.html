About ResearchSupportDesk

We are a team of dedicated academic professionals committed to empowering students and researchers across Nigeria with expert guidance, comprehensive support, and innovative solutions for their research journey.

Our Story

ResearchSupportDesk was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between ambitious students and successful research outcomes. Dr. Olaolorunpo Olorunfemi holds the distinguished position of Associate Professor and is a leading authority in multiple areas of health research, contributing significantly to advancements in the field.

Having supervised numerous undergraduate and graduate students, Dr. Olaolorunpo Olorunfemi observed that many brilliant minds struggled not with their ideas, but with the technical aspects of research methodology, data analysis, and academic writing. This insight led to the creation of ResearchSupportDesk.

Today, we have grown into a comprehensive research support platform, helping students from over 50 universities across Nigeria achieve their academic goals. Our success is measured not just in completed projects, but in the confidence and skills our clients gain throughout their research journey.
